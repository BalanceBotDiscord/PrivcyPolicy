1. Introduction

BalanceBot#8309 (“the Bot”) is a Discord economy bot that provides virtual currency features such as /balance, /daily, and /addmoney. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

  • Discord User IDs — to uniquely identify accounts for balances.
  • Economy Data — virtual balances, daily claims, streaks, and transaction records necessary to run the economy system.
  • Server/Channel IDs — only as needed to execute commands and store per-server settings.

We do not collect private messages, real-world personal data, or sensitive categories of information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Operate the economy system (store/update balances and rewards).
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or exploits of economy commands.
  • Improve stability and diagnose issues (e.g., minimal log entries for errors).

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Economy data is stored in a database tied to your Discord User ID.
  • We retain data while the bot is active in your server and for as long as needed for the economy features.
  • You may request deletion of your economy data at any time (see Section 7).

5. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or rent your data. Data is only shared if required by law, to enforce Discord’s Terms/Guidelines, or with infrastructure providers strictly to run the bot.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is intended for Discord users who meet Discord’s minimum age requirements. If you believe data was collected from someone under the applicable age, contact us to remove it.

7. Your Rights & Requests

  • Access — Ask what economy data we store for your User ID.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your economy data.
  • Correction — Report errors in your stored balance/records.

To make a request, contact: Imranrayyanshaikh123@gamil.com and include your Discord User ID.
